# 04 — Creative

Hooks, angles, ad formats, and creative testing — **the primary paid-social skill in 2026.**

**Why this folder matters most going forward:** since Meta removed granular targeting (Jan 15, 2026), *creative is the targeting* — the algorithm finds your buyers based on who responds to your ads. Whoever ships the most good, varied creative wins. Top brands ship **200–300 net-new concepts/month**; your edge is building an [AI-assisted pipeline](../06-automation/02-creative-pipeline.md) to approach that solo.

Key vocabulary you'll build out:
- **Hook** — the first 3 seconds. Wins or loses everything. Measured by *hook rate* (3s views ÷ impressions).
- **Angle** — the *reason this product matters to this person*. One product → many angles → many concepts.
- **Format** — UGC, static, testimonial, demo, founder-to-camera, etc.
- **Concept vs. variant** — a new concept is a new idea; a variant is a tweak. Meta punishes lazy variants (same image, new text = higher CPMs). Ship concepts.
